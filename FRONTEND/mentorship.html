Mentorship Program

One-on-one guidance from experienced leaders to accelerate your growth in leadership, technology & public service.

What is the Mentorship Program?

The Mentorship Program pairs motivated students with experienced professionals across technology, governance, and community leadership. Mentors provide career guidance, skills coaching, networking support, and project-based advice tailored to each mentee.

Who should join?

  • Students or early-career professionals interested in AI, IT, governance, or community leadership.
  • Those seeking personalized guidance, internships, or help building project portfolios.
  • Candidates committed to 3â€“6 months of active participation.

Program structure

  • Duration: 3 months (option to extend to 6 months)
  • Format: Bi-weekly mentor sessions + monthly workshops + capstone project
  • Delivery: Online / in-person (location-dependent)

Sample curriculum & activities

  • Goal-setting & career planning
  • Technical upskilling (AI fundamentals, coding, cyber hygiene)
  • Leadership labs: public speaking, policy writing, community organizing
  • Internship placement support and portfolio reviews

How to join

  1. Fill the application form: Apply here (or email info@vahedatrust.org with subject "Mentorship Application").
  2. Shortlist & interview: selected applicants will be invited for a short interview.
  3. Match with mentor: successful candidates are matched with a mentor and receive onboarding details.
